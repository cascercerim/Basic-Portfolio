About Me

I'm Maria. I am a mom of two. I have a husband who I sometimes think is the most amazing guy in the world. Because he is always so supportive. Guys, I got that lucky. He has been my rock throughout this process. I took all of my prereqs in nursing. I stopped, had my son. By the time I thought about going back for nursing, a lot of my prereqs expired. Plus I wasn't thrilled with the idea of 4 years of school, especially with having a family. My husband always said, if he could go back to school it would be for computers. He being the guy who went to the University of Delaware, started for nursing. Took someone off a bed-pan during his clinical. Decided that was one of the scariest things he's ever done in his life. Switched to Dietetics and is now an Insurance Agent. He always says a degree is just a piece of paper.

I agreed with that to some extent. I do wish earlier in life I had gone to school. But I am grateful I skipped out of wracking up a lot of debt. I thought later in life I would possibly be going to college with my kids. That thought terrified me. I decided to research the types of computer jobs. Types of schooling for computers. Computer science degree's, basically anything under the sun with the word computer in it. I stumbled onto coding. I was shocked finding out you didn't need a degree in this field.
